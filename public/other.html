Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)

PMAY aims to provide affordable housing to urban and rural poor households by offering subsidies and financial support for constructing or purchasing a house.

Start Date: 2015-06-25
End Date: 2024-12-31
Eligibility:
  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • Income criteria vary for urban and rural areas.
  • Prioritized for first-time house buyers.
  • Resident of Maharashtra.
Official Details

Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)

MGNREGA guarantees 100 days of wage employment per year to rural households, aiming to improve the livelihood of rural citizens by creating sustainable rural assets.

Start Date: 2006-02-02
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Rural households in Maharashtra.
  • Available to all adult members.
  • No formal educational qualifications required.
Official Details

Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY)

DDU-GKY enhances the employability of rural youth through skill training and capacity building, helping them secure gainful employment.

Start Date: 2014-09-25
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Rural youth aged between 15 and 35 years.
  • Basic educational qualifications as per the course requirements.
  • Primarily targeted at economically weaker sections.
Official Details

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

PMJDY is a financial inclusion program providing affordable access to banking services and financial products, particularly for those from economically weaker sections.

Start Date: 2014-08-28
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Open to all Indian citizens.
  • Focus on enrolling those without existing bank accounts.
  • Emphasis on rural and urban poor.
Official Details

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

PMMY provides loans to micro and small enterprises to support income-generating activities, thereby empowering small business owners and entrepreneurs.

Start Date: 2015-04-08
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Micro and small enterprises in Maharashtra.
  • Must have a viable business plan.
  • Applicant should possess a valid Aadhaar and bank account.
Official Details

Maharashtra State Skill Development Schemes

These schemes aim to enhance employability by providing vocational training and skill development programs tailored to local market needs.

Start Date: 2017-07-01
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Residents of Maharashtra, especially youth.
  • Applicants should have basic educational qualifications.
  • Must meet the specific requirements of the training program.
Official Details

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan - Maharashtra

An initiative aimed at ensuring clean and healthy surroundings, this scheme focuses on improving sanitation, waste management, and overall public health.

Start Date: 2014-10-02
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Open to all residents participating in community cleanliness drives.
  • Focus on schools, communities, and local bodies.
  • Implementation through state government bodies and local administrations.
Official Details
