Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

Ayushman Bharat provides a health cover of up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization, protecting economically vulnerable families from high medical expenses.

Start Date: September 2018
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Belong to the economically vulnerable sections as per government criteria
  • Possess a valid Ayushman Card issued by the government
  • Indian citizen residing in Maharashtra
Official Details

Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY)

Aimed at correcting regional imbalances in affordable tertiary healthcare services and augmenting medical education.

Start Date: March 2006
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Open to Indian citizens
  • Focus on underserved regions
  • Referral-based for tertiary care
Official Details

Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi (RAN)

Provides financial help to BPL families for life-threatening diseases requiring hospital treatment.

Start Date: 1997
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • BPL families with proof
  • Diagnosis of serious disease
  • Treatment at government hospitals
Official Details

National Health Mission (NHM)

Provides accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare especially in rural areas.

Start Date: April 2005
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Available to rural and low-income populations
  • Focus on maternal and child health
  • State and district level implementation
Official Details

Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS)

Healthcare facilities to central government employees, pensioners and dependents in CGHS-covered cities.

Start Date: 1954
End Date: Ongoing
Eligibility:
  • Central govt. employees and pensioners
  • Eligible family members
  • In CGHS-covered cities only
Official Details
