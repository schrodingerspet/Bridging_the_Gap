Maharashtra Health Schemes

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

Ayushman Bharat provides a health cover of up to INR 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization. This scheme aims to protect economically vulnerable families from high medical expenses.

Eligibility:
  • Belong to the economically vulnerable sections.
  • Possess an Ayushman Card issued by the government.
  • Must be an Indian citizen residing in Maharashtra.

Mukhyamantri Amrut Niramay Yojana

This state-specific scheme provides free medical treatment and preventive healthcare services at government hospitals across Maharashtra, ensuring that quality healthcare is accessible to all citizens.

Eligibility:
  • Permanent resident of Maharashtra.
  • Open to all citizens irrespective of income.
  • Enrollment may be required at designated government hospitals.
Official Details

Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme

This scheme offers comprehensive health insurance coverage for residents of Maharashtra, covering a wide range of medical treatments to reduce the financial burden of health emergencies.

Eligibility:
  • Must be a resident of Maharashtra.
  • Priority given to families below a specified income threshold.
  • Enrollment through local government offices or hospitals.
Official Details

Maharashtra National Health Mission (MNHM)

MNHM works to enhance healthcare infrastructure and services across the state, focusing on both rural and urban areas. The mission aims to provide accessible and quality healthcare for all citizens.

Eligibility:
  • Open to all residents of Maharashtra.
  • Focus on delivering services in underprivileged areas.
  • No direct application process; benefits are availed at government health centers.
