Maharashtra Health Schemes

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

Ayushman Bharat provides a health cover of up to ₹5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization. It is designed to protect economically vulnerable families from high medical expenses.

Eligibility:
  • Belong to the economically vulnerable sections as per government criteria
  • Possess a valid Ayushman Card issued by the government
  • Indian citizen residing in Maharashtra

Mukhyamantri Amrut Niramay Yojana

This scheme offers comprehensive free medical treatment, including diagnostic services and surgeries, to ensure that quality healthcare is accessible to all citizens of Maharashtra.

Eligibility:
  • Permanent resident of Maharashtra
  • Open to all income groups
  • Services available at designated government hospitals

Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme

This scheme provides comprehensive health insurance coverage for residents of Maharashtra, aiming to reduce out-of-pocket expenses during health emergencies by offering financial support for medical treatments.

Eligibility:
  • Must be a resident of Maharashtra
  • Priority given to families below a defined income threshold
  • Enrollment via government channels
Official Details

Maharashtra National Health Mission (MNHM)

MNHM aims to improve healthcare infrastructure and service delivery across both rural and urban areas of Maharashtra, ensuring accessible and quality health care for all citizens.

Eligibility:
  • Open to all residents of Maharashtra
  • Focus on delivering services in underserved areas
  • No direct application process; benefits are availed at government health centers
