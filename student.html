Maharashtra Student Scholarship Schemes

Govt. of India Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students, Maharashtra

This scholarship helps Scheduled Caste students continue their post-matric education by covering tuition fees, maintenance, and other education-related expenses.

Eligibility:
  • Must be from the Scheduled Caste (SC) category
  • Passed Class 10
  • Family income not exceeding ₹2.5 lakh annually
  • Resident of Maharashtra

Post Matric Scholarship for ST Students, Maharashtra

This scheme offers financial assistance for Scheduled Tribe students to pursue education beyond matriculation, easing their path to higher studies.

Eligibility:
  • Must be from the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category
  • Passed previous exams
  • Family income not exceeding ₹2.5 lakh annually
  • Resident of Maharashtra

Tuition Fee Reimbursement for OBC Students, Maharashtra

This initiative reduces financial strain on OBC students by reimbursing tuition and examination fees, making higher education more accessible.

Eligibility:
  • Must be from the OBC category
  • Family income below the prescribed threshold
  • Enrolled in a post-matriculation institution
  • Resident of Maharashtra
Official Details

Merit-Based Scholarship for Higher Education, Maharashtra

This scholarship rewards high-performing students with financial aid, supporting their undergraduate and postgraduate studies in Maharashtra.

Eligibility:
  • High academic performance as per set criteria
  • Enrolled in a recognized higher education institution
  • Resident of Maharashtra
  • Meets additional performance requirements as defined by the scheme
