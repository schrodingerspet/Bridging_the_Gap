Maharashtra Student Scholarship Schemes

Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students, Maharashtra

This scholarship provides financial assistance to Scheduled Caste students pursuing post-matriculation or post-secondary education to enable them to complete their education.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC) category.
  • Passed Class 10 examination.
  • Family's annual income should not exceed ₹2.5 lakh.

Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for ST Students, Maharashtra

This scheme aims to provide financial assistance to Scheduled Tribe students studying at post-matriculation levels to support their educational endeavors.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category.
  • Passed the previous examination.
  • Family's annual income should not exceed ₹2.5 lakh.

Tuition Fees and Examination Fees for OBC Students, Maharashtra

This scheme reimburses tuition and examination fees for Other Backward Class (OBC) students to reduce financial barriers in pursuing higher education.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to the Other Backward Class (OBC) category.
  • Studying in Class 11 to postgraduation level.
  • Family's annual income should not exceed ₹8 lakh.

Rajarshi Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship

This merit-based scholarship aims to encourage students from the Denotified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis), Nomadic Tribes, and Special Backward Classes to pursue higher education by providing financial assistance.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to VJNT, OBC, or SBC category.
  • Studying in Class 11 or 12.
  • No academic gap during the course.

Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Foreign Scholarship Scheme for SC Students

This scheme provides scholarships to Scheduled Caste and Neo-Buddhist students who have secured admission for special studies abroad, aiming to promote higher education on a global platform.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Belong to Scheduled Caste or Neo-Buddhist community.
  • Secured admission for studies abroad.
  • Meet the income limit and other criteria as specified by the Social Justice and Special Assistance Department.
