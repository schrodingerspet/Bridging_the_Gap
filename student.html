Maharashtra Student Scholarship Schemes

Govt. of India Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students, Maharashtra

This scheme offers financial assistance to Scheduled Caste students pursuing post-matriculation or post-secondary education. It covers tuition fees, maintenance allowances, and other education-related expenses.

Eligibility:
  • Must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC) category.
  • Passed Class 10 with eligible marks.
  • Family annual income should not exceed ₹2.5 lakh.
  • Applicant must be a resident of Maharashtra.

Post Matric Scholarship for ST Students, Maharashtra

This scheme supports Scheduled Tribe students by providing financial aid for post-matriculation education, enabling them to overcome economic barriers and pursue higher studies.

Eligibility:
  • Must belong to the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category.
  • Passed the previous examination.
  • Family income not exceeding ₹2.5 lakh per annum.
  • Resident of Maharashtra.

Tuition Fee Reimbursement for OBC Students, Maharashtra

This scheme is designed to reduce the financial burden on Other Backward Class students by reimbursing tuition and examination fees, helping them focus on their studies.

Eligibility:
  • Must belong to the OBC category.
  • Family income should be below the specified threshold.
  • Enrolled in a recognized post-matriculation institution.
  • Resident of Maharashtra.

Merit-Based Scholarship for Higher Education, Maharashtra

This merit-based scholarship rewards high-achieving students across various disciplines by providing financial assistance to ease the cost of higher education in Maharashtra.

Eligibility:
  • Must demonstrate excellent academic performance.
  • Enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate program.
  • Resident of Maharashtra.
  • Subject to specific performance criteria as defined by the scheme.
