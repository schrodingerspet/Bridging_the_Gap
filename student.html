Schemes for Students

Government of India Post-Matric Scholarship for SC Students, Maharashtra

This scholarship provides essential financial assistance to Scheduled Caste students to help them complete their post-matriculation or post-secondary education.

Eligibility:
  • Must belong to the Scheduled Caste (SC) category.
  • Passed the Class 10 examination.
  • Family annual income should not exceed ₹2.5 lakh.

Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for ST Students, Maharashtra

Designed for Scheduled Tribe students, this scheme offers financial support for continuing education beyond matriculation, ensuring that higher studies are accessible.

Eligibility:
  • Must belong to the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category.
  • Passed the previous examination.
  • Family income should not exceed ₹2.5 lakh per annum.

Tuition and Examination Fee Reimbursement for OBC Students, Maharashtra

This initiative aims to alleviate financial burdens by reimbursing tuition and examination fees for Other Backward Class (OBC) students, ensuring a smoother academic journey.

Eligibility:
  • Must belong to the OBC category.
  • Family income should be below the specified limit.
  • Applicant should be pursuing post-matriculation education.

Merit-Based Scholarship for Higher Education, Maharashtra

Aimed at fostering academic excellence, this scholarship rewards high-performing students with financial aid, encouraging them to pursue higher education without economic constraints.

Eligibility:
  • High academic performance (as per set criteria).
  • Must be a resident of Maharashtra.
  • Applicable for students enrolled in undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
