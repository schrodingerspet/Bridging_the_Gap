Student Schemes - Maharashtra

Rajarshi Shahu Maharaj Scholarship

Eligibility:

  • Domicile of Maharashtra
  • Belong to EBC category
  • Admitted to professional courses (e.g., Engineering, Medical)
  • Family income ≤ Rs.8.00 lakh

Benefits:

  • 50% of tuition and exam fees if income ≤ Rs.2.50 lakh
  • 100% of tuition and exam fees if income > Rs.2.50 lakh but ≤ Rs.8.00 lakh

Application Process:

  • Apply online via MahaDBT portal
  • Submit income certificate, mark sheets, etc.

Post Matric SC Scholarship

Eligibility:

  • Belong to SC category
  • Domicile of Maharashtra
  • Studying in post-matric courses
  • Family income ≤ Rs.2.50 lakh

Benefits:

  • Full tuition fee reimbursement
  • Maintenance allowance
  • Allowances for study tours, etc.

Application Process:

  • Apply through MahaDBT portal
  • Upload caste certificate, income certificate, etc.
